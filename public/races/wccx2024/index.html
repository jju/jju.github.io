    western cyclocross challenge 2024

    September 14, 2024
    cycling
    cx, races, canmore nordic centre, canada, canmore, rmcc, cyclemeisters, bow cycle, western challenge, masters, junior
    • Tech Guide
    • Location: Canmore Nordic Centre Canmore
    • Course: stravalink

    Western Challenge CX. This race is done in age groups to get points for call-ups for Nationals and what have you so it isn’t part of the Alberta Cup CX race series.
