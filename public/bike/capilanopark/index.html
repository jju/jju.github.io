capilano park

#cx  #places  #capilano park  #mtb  #edmonton 
September 15, 2023

Capilano Park is a park in the city of Edmonton, where I wouldn’t actually expect a cx race to work, but it does.

As far as the singletrack in the park goes, this is where you connect up with Goldbar Park and the cool trails in there. I generally prefer riding Eastbound.
